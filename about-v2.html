ABOUT US

Our Story

Founded on Passion,
Driven by Art

LensLuxe Photography began in 2014 with a simple belief: every moment deserves to be captured beautifully.

Founded by award-winning photographer Alexandra Hart, what started as a one-person passion project has evolved into a full-service photography studio with a team of talented artists. We've had the privilege of documenting thousands of life's most precious moments.

Our approach combines technical excellence with artistic vision, ensuring that every photograph tells a compelling story that will be treasured for generations.
